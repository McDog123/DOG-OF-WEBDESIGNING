About Dalmatian's

Diet

feed your Dalmatian a good quality diet that includes lamb, poultry, and eggs, which are lower in purine. If your dog develops bladder stones, it may need even stricter protein restrictions to reduce the risk.

Behaviour

Reserved and dignified, Dals can be aloof with strangers and are dependable watchdogs. With their preferred humans, Dals are bright, loyal, and loving house dogs.

Looks

The Dalmatian is a graceful, medium-sized dog with a sleek, muscular body. The tail is fairly long and has a slight, upward curve. The coat is short and dense, with brown or black spots on white. The Dalmatian sheds heavily, even though the coat is short.
